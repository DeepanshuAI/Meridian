Hotel Amenities

Everything you came to slow down for

Home/Amenities

Wellness

The Salt Water Spa

Five treatment rooms, a private hammam, and a saltwater soaking pool fed by the same Atlantic just beyond the dunes. Treatments draw on local botanicals — sea fennel, kelp, rosemary from the kitchen garden.

  • Five private treatment rooms
  • Saltwater soaking pool & hammam
  • Couples' suite with private terrace
Open daily · 7am – 9pm

Swim

Infinity Pool

A 25-metre infinity edge appears to spill straight into the Atlantic. Heated year-round, with a shallow sun shelf for lounging and a quieter adults-only section past the cabana row.

  • Heated year-round, 25-metre infinity edge
  • Poolside bar & cabana service
  • Adults-only section
Open daily · 6am – 10pm

Move

Fitness Studio

A full-glass studio facing the dunes, equipped for strength and cardio, with sunrise yoga on the lawn three mornings a week and a rotating roster of visiting instructors in season.

  • Free weights, cardio & functional zone
  • Sunrise yoga on the lawn, Tue/Thu/Sat
  • Personal training on request
Open 24 hours

Gather

Conference Hall

A daylit hall for up to 80 guests, with breakout rooms, full AV, and a private terrace for breaks. Equally suited to a board retreat or a small wedding reception.

  • Seats up to 80, with two breakout rooms
  • Full AV & high-speed connectivity
  • Private terrace for breaks & receptions
By reservation

Arrive

Airport Transfer

A private car meets every suite and penthouse guest at arrivals, no scheduling required — just send your flight number ahead. Transfers for other room types can be arranged through the concierge.

  • Complimentary for suite & penthouse guests
  • Flight tracking, no scheduling needed
  • Available for all guests on request
24-hour availability

Stay Connected

Complimentary Wi-Fi

Fibre-backed wireless throughout the property, including the beach and pool deck — fast enough for a video call from a lounge chair, should that be the kind of vacation you're having.

  • Property-wide coverage, including the beach
  • Fibre connection, no speed caps
  • Business centre with private call booths
Always on
