Chef Lucia Marin

Meet the Chef

Cooking with the tide

Lucia trained in Lisbon and San Sebastián before returning to the Atlantic coast she grew up on. Her menus follow the boats: what comes in at dawn decides what's on the table by dusk. Nothing is flown in that could be caught, grown, or foraged within a morning's drive.
